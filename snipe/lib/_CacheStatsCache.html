package snipe.lib

typedef _CacheStatsCache

Data block cache stats.

Properties

databaseCalls:Float

Amount of check() calls.

databaseReads:Float

Amount of database reads.

databaseWrites:Float

Amount of database writes.

getCacheHits:Float

Amount of cache hits on get().

getCacheMisses:Float

Amount of cache misses on get().

getCalls:Float

Amount of get() calls.

getNotFound:Float

Amount of get()s resulting in 'notFound'.

getSameVersion:Float

Amount of get()s resulting in 'sameVersion'.

lockAttempts:Float

Amount of attempts to acquire data block lock.

lockCalls:Float

Amount of lock() calls.

lockFailures:Float

Amount of lock failures.

segmentCalls:Float

Amount of getSegment() calls.

segmentHits:Float

Amount of immediate segment hits in getSegment().

segmentMisses:Float

Amount of fully missed segment checks in getSegment().
